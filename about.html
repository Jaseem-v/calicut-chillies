Who are we?

We’re an absolute leader in the Cuisine-themed Catering services.

Our team of experienced chefs and event planners work closely with each client to create a unique culinary experience tailored to their specific needs and preferences. Whether you're planning a small intimate gathering or a grand gala event, we have the expertise and resources to make it a truly memorable occasion.

Our Mission

Chillies catering and event management believes in team spirit and commitment. Two decades of experience is our best teacher and the work culture we acquired through time is our asset. A committed group of people who are ready to be at the service of our client, anytime anywhere is the backbone of Calicut chillies. Experienced and ever experimental experts in the kitchen, highly creative, practical and enthusiastic team in event management who makes everything possible works the best for us. The happy face of the client makes our effort and hard work worthwhile. A committed team for your happy occasion is our promise to you.
